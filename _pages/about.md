I am a Research Scientist at Meta Reality Labs Research, where I focus on the intersection of physics-based simulation and machine learning. My current work involves developing differentiable simulation frameworks for anatomically-inspired human models and building efficient, neural-based pipelines for real-time avatar dynamics.

Before joining Meta, I spent several years as a Simulation Developer at SideFX, contributing to the core FLIP-based fluid solvers in the VFX software package Houdini. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, where my research in fluid simulation and numerical PDEs earned the [2021 Alain Fournier Dissertation Award](https://graphicsinterface.org/awards/alain-fournier/ryan-goldade/).

My research has been featured in [Motherboard](https://www.vice.com/en_us/article/kzkmwy/computer-animation-physics-christopher-batty-asmr), [80 Level](https://80.lv/articles/research-adaptive-framework-for-fluid-simulation-002sgr-006sdf/), the SIGGRAPH [2019](https://youtu.be/EhDr3Rs5fTU?t=16) and [2020](https://youtu.be/jYdMKdRUq_8?t=63) Technical Papers Preview, and Two Minute Papers [2019](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K940MNp7V8M&ab_channel=TwoMinutePapers) and [2020](https://youtu.be/MD_k3p4MH-A). My PhD research has been incorporated into Houdini as the air incompressibility features (16.5) and adaptive viscosity (18.5). I also designed several new features for the Houdini fluid simulation pipeline, such as the surface tension and suction fluid tools (16.0), narrow-band FLIP (16.5, 17.0, 17.5), air incompressibility (16.5), new ocean simulation tools (guided volumes and boundary layer) (16.0), and adaptive pressure projection (18.0).

Research & Technical Interests
======

* **Differentiable Simulation**: Integrating physics engines into optimization pipelines (PyTorch/C++) for high-fidelity body and hand tracking.
* **Neural Physics**: Designing autoregressive ML models (VAEs, Graph NNs) for stable, extreme-performance secondary dynamics on compute-limited hardware.
* **Fluid Dynamics**: Expertise in very-high-resolution fluid simulation, vortex-particle methods, and large-scale parallel numerical solvers.
* **Avatar Synthesis**: Creating drivable, physics-based rigs with active elastic materials for realistic digital humans.
